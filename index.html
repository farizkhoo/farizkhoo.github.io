Not your average tour guides

We started our tour goup in 2016 curating tours for our friends who were visiting from around the world. Since then, we have expanded our tours into a full fledged business.
We avoid tourist traps in favor of sights that you really want to see - a motorcycle ride around "kampongs", a visit to a serene beach in the East Coast and the list goes on. Pretty much anything that offers you a glimpse of what it would be like to experience live in this country with a local friend by your side. Our tour guides not only have street cred, but most of all they are excited to show you Malaysia like never before.
